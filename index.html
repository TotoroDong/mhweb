About My Research

My research seeks to answer a central question: How can organizations and the public leverage emerging media technologies (e.g., AI-driven chatbots, virtual influencers, immersive media)
to communicate effectively and achieve meaningful outcomes for the social good, particularly in health promotion and crisis management?

My primary research methodologies consist of quantitative approaches, including online and offline experiments, surveys, content analysis, and computational analysis.

Research Interest: Strategic Communication Public Relations Emerging Media Effects Health Communication Crisis Management Persuasive Communication
